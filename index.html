About Us


Welcome to Roti Rozaana, where we believe in the power of food to bring hope and nourishment to those in need.
Our mission is simple yet profound: to bridge the gap between surplus food and hunger by connecting donors with those who are hungry. In a world where millions go to bed hungry every night, we recognize the urgent need to redistribute excess food to those who need it most. Our platform serves as a vital link in this process, facilitating the donation of surplus food from restaurants, grocery stores, events, and individuals to local food banks, shelters, and community organizations.
